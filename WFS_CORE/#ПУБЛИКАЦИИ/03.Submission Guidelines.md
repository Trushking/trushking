
## 📜 Submission Guidelines for the “WORLDWIDE FAMILY OF SAINTS” (#WFS) Publication on Medium

**Dear friends, authors, seekers, researchers!**

The Medium publication of the Charitable Foundation “WORLD FAMILY OF SAINTS” (#WFS) is open to your thoughts, discoveries, and inspiration. #WFS believes that every person can awaken their divine nature, and invites you to explore this path together — with joy, respect, and care for one another.

### 1. What may be discussed from the heart?

The #WFS Laboratory welcomes materials that warm the heart and clarify the mind:

- **Science and Consciousness:** simple and profound stories about how attention works. How thoughts affect health and youth — share your experience and research.
- **Spiritual Traditions:** living words about Advaita Vedanta, the teachings of realized masters (Nisargadatta Maharaj and others). What non‑duality is and how it feels in everyday life.
- **Charity and Service:** your stories of helping others, how supporting others transforms ourselves. Accounts of retreats, lectures, and good deeds.
- **Economics of Attention:** the impact of mental noise on business, management, and law. How caring for the inner world increases efficiency and brings joy.
- **Wise Humor:** light, kind stories in the spirit of #HohmaKino that help the reader not to freeze in seriousness and to see absurdity without judgment.

### 2. A little about “inner interlocutors” (archetypes)

In the #WFS Mission, we study that each of us carries certain inner roles — archetypes. They can help or hinder clear perception. In texts, they sometimes appear unconsciously. There is no need to fight them; we learn to notice and soften them. Here are some of them:

- **Eggplant (Baklazhan):** presses with authority, demands obedience. Its voice: “You must…”, “It’s obvious…”
- **Bore (Zanuda):** analyzes endlessly, gets lost in details, misses the essence.
- **Mole (Krot):** digs for himself, suspects everyone of dishonesty.
- **Draft (Skvoznyak):** spreads rumors, sows panic.
- **Pheasant (Fazan):** wants to appear better than he is, loves praise.
- **Slug (Tyufyak):** passive, afraid to take responsibility.
- **Adventurer (Avantyurist):** pushes for unjustified risk, chases easy gain.

If you notice that any of these voices have appeared in your article — it’s okay. The important thing is that you see it. #VSS does not demand perfect purity, but it values awareness. Gradually, with practice, one can learn to speak from oneself — true, kind, and clear.

### 3. A few wishes for your texts

- **Be yourself.** Sincerity matters more than perfection. The #WFS Laboratory does not demand an ideal style — it values a living soul.
- **Rely on facts, but do not fear feelings.** If you have verifiable data — great. If you have experience and emotions — share them openly.
- **Avoid coldness.** Try to write without excessive impersonality. The pronouns “I” and “you” are not forbidden if they sound natural and respectful.
- **Length:** 800 to 3000 words — enough to cover the topic without tiring the reader.

### 4. How to submit your material?

Write to the #VSS Laboratory at **`siddhanathadas@gmail.com`** with the subject line: **“Publication on Medium / #WFS”**. Attach your text in a convenient format (Word, Google Docs, or just in the body of the email). In a few sentences, tell us what your article is about and why it might be of interest to the #VSS Laboratory.

The #VSS staff will reply within **5–7 business days** — always kindly and with respect for your work.

### 5. Right to light editing

The administration may slightly soften or clarify wording to better align the text with the overall spirit of the publication. If changes are significant, #VSS staff will always coordinate them with you.

### 6. When we may decline

We do not publish material that contains:
- insults, aggression, calls for violence;
- propaganda of hatred on any grounds;
- blatant advertising with no benefit to readers;
- deliberate falsehood or distortion of facts.

But even if the topic does not fit, #WFS staff are always ready to explain the reason and wish you well.
