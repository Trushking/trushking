4.2. Benefits from implementing #BT  

Individual: 
- Income growth of 30% (bonuses, promotions, fewer mistakes).  
- Expense reduction of 20% (budget optimisation, avoiding impulse spending).  
- Savings on healthcare (early diagnostics) – $5,000/year.  
- Total net benefit: $70,000/year (starting from $85,000 losses → the balance becomes positive).

Business (ROI):  
ROI formula (from Appendix 3 of SAT‑CHIT’s work):  
ROI = (P × E – C) / C × 100%, where  
- P = losses from the absence of #BT (% of turnover),  
- E = efficiency of loss reduction (50–70%),  
- C = implementation cost (2–5% of turnover).

Example (turnover $5 million):  
- P = 20% → $1 million  
- C = 4% → $200,000  
- E = 60%  
- Savings = $600,000  
- ROI = ($600,000 – $200,000) / $200,000 = 200%  

For small businesses, ROI reaches 900%.

State:  
- Reduction of corruption by 50% → $15 billion/year.  
- Increase in government efficiency by 20% → $50 billion/year (GDP growth).  
- Reduction of crime by 10% → $20 billion/year.  
- Total direct savings: $85 billion/year, plus indirect effects (better health, lower litigation costs, reduced social tension) – up to $200 billion/year.
