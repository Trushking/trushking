1.1. Definition of #BT within #WFS  
(repeated from the introduction, but compressed, #FL)

#BT – a verifiable statement 1 (TRUE) or 0 (FALSE). Requirements:  
- Legitimacy of the source (not anonymous, signature, #SS).

Not #BT: opinions, forecasts, rumours, anonymous data, “it seems to me”.

1.2. Connection of #BT with uncertainty and archetypes  

Any uncertainty automatically activates destructive archetypes. Mechanism:  
- Uncertainty = absence of a clear 1/0.  
- The brain cannot tolerate uncertainty and fills it with “guesses”.  
- Guesses are a breeding ground for Draft (paranoia), Pheasant (excuses), Mole (inertia) and others.  

#BT calmly and factually eliminates uncertainty. How? By dividing a complex task into a packet of small #BT steps. Each small step has an answer 1 or 0. The overall solution – Axe (to cut off the superfluous beautifully).

When the level of #CLARITY ≥ 9/10 (i.e. in a society where #BT is widespread), crime decreases. Why? Uncertainty disappears – the Draft archetype loses energy. Savings from crime reduction (expenditure on police, courts, prisons) – tens of billions of dollars per year for a large state.

1.5. #BT as a tool for decomposing complex tasks  

A complex task is often perceived as uncertainty (“how to do it?”), which activates the Bore archetype (endless analysis) and Draft (fear of failure). #BT offers an algorithm: break the task into a sequence of binary steps.

Example: implementing #SS in a company.  
- Step 1: Has the service string been created? (1/0)  
- Step 2: Has a training for employees been conducted? (1/0)  
- Step 3: Are the metrics #ISK_DEV and #AVG_CHECK recorded? (1/0)  
- Step 4: Is there a weekly audit? (1/0)  

Each step is binary. Uncertainty disappears. Archetypes lose their power.
